Calendar Todo

Privacy Policy

Calendar ToDo ("the App") is a read-only calendar viewer that lets you mark events as done. This policy explains how information is handled.

Last updated: {{ site.data.legal.last_updated }} Language: English
Japanese version Legal index

1. Information We Access and Store

  • Calendar data: event titles, dates/times, calendars, and identifiers accessed via EventKit to display events and compute stats. The App does not edit your calendar events.
  • Completion data: done status, history, and diagnostic records stored locally in Core Data.
  • iCloud sync (optional): if iCloud is enabled for the App, the above data is synced to your private iCloud database via CloudKit.
  • Retention: data remains on your device and in iCloud until you delete the App or remove iCloud data for the App.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality such as event display, completion tracking, analytics, and relinking.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • No tracking or advertising.
  • Apple services used: iCloud/CloudKit for sync and StoreKit for subscriptions.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment information.

5. Your Choices

  • You can revoke Calendar access in iOS Settings.
  • You can disable iCloud for the App in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the App to remove local data.
